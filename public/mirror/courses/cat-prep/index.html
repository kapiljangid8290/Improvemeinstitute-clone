CAT4 Test Prep

Ages 7-15 | Cognitive Assessment

Structured preparation for the CAT4 Cognitive Abilities Test — the assessment required by every private school in Dubai. We build your child's confidence across all four reasoning batteries so they perform at their best.

Ideally, begin CAT4 preparation 3-6 months before your child's school application deadline. This allows time for diagnostic testing, targeted practice, and mock examinations.

Structure

Programme Structure

Step-by-step preparation process

01

Hour 1 — Introduction to CAT4

What the test measures and how it is scored. Overview of the four batteries: Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning, Quantitative Reasoning, Spatial Ability.

02

Hour 2 — Verbal Reasoning

Word analogies, odd one out, sentence completion. Timed practice with exam-condition technique.

03

Hour 3 — Non-Verbal Reasoning

Series completion, matrices, pattern recognition. Visual strategies and elimination technique.

04

Hour 4 — Quantitative Reasoning

Number sequences, data interpretation, word problems. Mental maths strategies under timed conditions.

05

Hour 5 — Spatial Ability

2D/3D shape rotation, nets, spatial sequences. Common error patterns and how to avoid them.

06

Hour 6 — Full Practice Test + Review

Simulated CAT4 test under timed conditions. Full debrief, score analysis, and targeted revision plan for each student.
