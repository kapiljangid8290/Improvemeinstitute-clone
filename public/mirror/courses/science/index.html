Two Stages, Two Approaches

Science at primary level (from Year 4) and science at Key Stage 3 demand very different things from your child. We structure our tutoring around what matters most at each stage — so your child gets exactly the right support at the right time.

Biology Topics

  • Skeletal and muscular systems, nutrition and digestion, gas exchange
  • Reproduction, photosynthesis, respiration, ecosystems
  • Genetics and evolution

Chemistry Topics

  • Particle model of matter, atoms, elements and compounds
  • Chemical reactions, the periodic table, acids and alkalis
  • Earth and atmosphere, energetics

Physics Topics

  • Energy transfers and calculations, forces and motion
  • Waves (sound and light), electricity and electromagnetism
  • Matter and particle model, space physics
