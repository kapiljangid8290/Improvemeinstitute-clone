WHAT WE DO

What is Educational Counselling?

Educational counselling provides expert guidance to students and families making important academic decisions. From choosing the right school and entrance exam preparation to selecting GCSE/IGCSE options and planning university applications, our counsellors help navigate the complex educational landscape in Dubai and internationally.

School Selection & Admissions

Finding the right fit and navigating application timelines.

Subject & Pathway Choices

GCSE, A-Level, and IB options aligned with long-term goals.

University Applications

UCAS strategy, personal statements, and course selection.

Academic Planning

Long-term strategy from primary through to university.
