English

English is the subject that parents find hardest to diagnose. A child who reads fluently can still struggle to write analytically. A student who passes comprehension exercises can fall apart when asked to write an essay under timed conditions. At Improve ME, we teach English across every level — from phonics and early reading at primary, through to A-Level and IB English Language — because the skills compound: a student who learns to construct an argument properly at KS3 will still be using that skill at university.
