What Children Learn at Key Stage 2

Skills that prepare for GL Assessments, 11+, and secondary school

Mathematics

  • Times tables mastery (2s through 12s) — the foundation of all future maths
  • Place value to 10,000 — understanding big numbers with confidence
  • Addition and subtraction with regrouping — mental and written methods
  • Introduction to fractions — halves, quarters, thirds, and equivalent fractions
  • Multiplication and division — moving beyond tables to multi-step problems
  • Shape, perimeter, and area — practical geometry that connects to real life
  • Data handling — reading and creating charts, tables, and graphs
  • Problem-solving — word problems and reasoning that build mathematical thinking

English

  • Reading with understanding — moving from decoding to genuinely comprehending texts
  • Vocabulary development — expanding the words children use and understand
  • Sentence construction — writing varied, interesting sentences with correct punctuation
  • Paragraph writing — structuring ideas logically with a beginning, middle, and end
  • Creative writing — stories, descriptions, and imaginative tasks that build confidence
  • Grammar essentials — nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, conjunctions in context
  • Spelling rules and patterns — building towards secure independent spelling
  • GL Assessment awareness — familiarisation with assessment formats from Year 3

Science (Living World · Materials & Matter · Forces & Energy)

  • Living World: Plants and what they need to grow; animals and their habitats; food chains and what eats what; the human body — bones, muscles, and keeping healthy
  • Materials & Matter: Properties of everyday materials; solids, liquids, and gases; why we use different materials for different purposes; simple changes and investigations
  • Forces & Energy: Pushes, pulls, and magnets; light and shadows; sound and vibration; simple circuits and electricity basics

Curriculum & Assessment

GL Assessments and readiness for secondary

At the end of Year 6, children in England (and many British curriculum schools in Dubai) sit GL Assessments in Reading, Mathematics, and Grammar, Punctuation and Spelling. Science is typically assessed by teacher assessment. These results help secondary schools understand each child's attainment and can inform setting and support in Year 7.

We support KS2 students with curriculum-aligned teaching and exam-style practice so they approach the tests with confidence. We also offer 11+ entrance exam preparation for families whose children are applying to selective schools.
