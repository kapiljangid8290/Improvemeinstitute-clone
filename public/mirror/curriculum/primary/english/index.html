OVERVIEW

What is Key Stage 2 English?

Key Stage 2 covers Years 3 to 6 — the core of your child's primary journey. At Improve ME, we teach KS2 English through our proprietary EngLit programme, which builds confident readers, skilled writers, and clear communicators across two focused stages: Lower KS2 (Years 3–4) and Upper KS2 (Years 5–6).

COURSE CONTENT

What Your Child Learns

Clear, age-appropriate learning with real progress you can see.

Reading & Comprehension

  • •Developing fluent, expressive reading
  • •Retrieving key information and making predictions using evidence
  • •Drawing inferences and explaining answers
  • •Analysing character development, figurative language, and authorial intent
  • •Comparing texts across genres and distinguishing fact, opinion, and bias

Writing & Composition

  • •Writing for different purposes and audiences
  • •Planning, drafting, and editing written work
  • •Descriptive and figurative language; narrative, recounts, letters, reports
  • •Discursive essays, balanced arguments, and persuasive writing
  • •11+ and selective school extended writing practice

Grammar, Spelling & Vocabulary

  • •Accurate sentence construction, punctuation, and sentence types
  • •Relative clauses, modal verbs, passive voice, and cohesion
  • •Spelling patterns and themed vocabulary building
  • •Point–Evidence–Explain–Analyse technique and CAT4-aligned critical thinking
