WHY IT MATTERS

Why Improve ME Matters in Primary School

What makes the difference in primary education — and how we deliver it.

We catch gaps before they grow

Primary school is where learning habits form — for better or worse. Our tutors identify exactly where your child is struggling and address it before it becomes a bigger problem at secondary level.

Small groups where every child is seen

From one-to-one at EYFS to small groups (average 2–3 students) at KS2, every session is sized so your child gets real attention. No lost questions, no waiting — just focused teaching at the right level.

We build on what school teaches — and go further

Our tutors work within the curriculum frameworks used across Dubai's leading schools, but sessions follow Improve ME's own structured programme — designed to stretch your child academically and develop well-rounded, confident learners, not just cover what's due next week in class.

Confidence, not just correct answers

We focus on how your child thinks, not just what they score. Students leave our sessions more willing to attempt problems, ask questions, and back themselves — skills that last well beyond primary school.
