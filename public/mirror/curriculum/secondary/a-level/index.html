PARENT GUIDE

What is A-Level? A Parent's Guide

Everything you need to know about the gold standard qualification for university entry

A-Levels (Advanced Levels) are the gold standard qualification for university entry in the UK and are widely recognised by top universities worldwide including those in the UAE, USA, Canada, and Australia.

Students typically study 3–4 A-Level subjects over two years (Year 12 and Year 13), sitting AS-Level exams at the end of Year 12 and full A-Level exams at the end of Year 13. The subjects chosen directly shape university options — making the right support critical.

At Improve ME, our A-Level tutors are subject specialists who know the exact mark schemes, essay structures, and exam techniques needed to achieve A and A* grades. We structure every lesson around what an A and A* answer actually looks like — not just content coverage.

WHY IT MATTERS

Why A-Levels Matter More Than Ever

A-Levels remain the primary route into top UK universities including Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, and UCL. In Dubai, many international school students sit A-Levels through schools following the British curriculum, and competition for top university places is fierce.

A single grade improvement at A-Level can be the difference between a conditional offer and a rejection. With universities raising their entry requirements, expert tuition at this level is not a luxury — it is essential.

We don't leave A-Level results to chance. Every session is designed to move your child closer to the grades that open university doors. Here's how.

01

Examiner-Style Marking

Our tutors mark work exactly as examiners do, using real mark schemes so students know precisely where marks are lost and won.

This level of feedback is critical at A-Level, where mark allocation is strict and every point counts toward the final grade.

02

Past Paper Mastery

We build exam confidence through structured past paper practice with timed conditions and detailed feedback.

03

Subject-Specialist Tutors

04

University Application Alignment

Tutoring at Improve ME supports not only exam grades but the broader academic profile that universities consider.
